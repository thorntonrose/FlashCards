# Demonstrative pronouns (this, that, etc.)

celui (-ci,-là)  | this one, that one (m.)
celle (-ci,-là)  | this one, that one (f.)
ceux  (-ci,-là)  | these, those (m., pl.)
celles (-ci,-là) | these, those (f., pl.)
ce               | this (before être)
ceci             | this (rarely spoken)
cela             | that
ça               | that (informal)
